The purpose of this project is to design and develop a web-based application that improves the current evaluation and feedback methods of the university. The project will be conducted in order to create an effective communication platform between students, teachers, and administrators allowing continuous improvement and awareness within the educational community.

Mission

To provide a comprehensive and systematic approach to gathering, analyzing, and utilizing feedback from students to continuously improve the quality of education and services at Cavite State University Bacoor City Campus.

Vision

To establish Cavite State University Bacoor City Campus as a model institution for excellence in education and service delivery through a culture of continuous improvement driven by effective feedback and evaluation mechanisms.

Goals

1. To collect and analyze feedback from students. 2. To use feedback to identify strengths, weaknesses, and opportunities for improvement. 3. To develop and implement action plans based on feedback to enhance the quality of education and services.
