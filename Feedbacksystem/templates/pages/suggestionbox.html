Virtual Suggestion Box

{% endblock header %} {% block feature %}
The Virtual Suggestion Box is your direct channel to share innovative suggestions and constructive feedback with Cavite State University Bacoor City Campus. Together, let's shape the future of our academic community.

Purpose

The Virtual Suggestion Box is designed to empower every member of our community, providing a platform to contribute ideas for positive change, propose solutions, and offer constructive feedback. Your insights are instrumental in the continuous enhancement of our university.

How It Works

Access the Virtual Suggestion Box: Log in to your student or staff account to access the platform. Select a Category: You have the option to select a specific category about the school if you prefer. Submit Your Suggestion: Share your ideas or feedback in a clear and concise manner. Track Progress: Follow the progress of your suggestions and see how they contribute to positive changes within our university. {% endblock feature %}
