Faculty Evaluation

{% endblock header %} {% block feature %}
In this dedicated feature, we strive to provide a transparent and insightful evaluation process for our esteemed faculty members. Your feedback plays a crucial role in enhancing the teaching and learning experience at our institution.

Purpose

Our faculty evaluation process is designed to ensure the continuous improvement of teaching quality, course content, and overall educational experience. By actively participating in this evaluation, you contribute to the ongoing refinement of our academic programs.

How It Works

Log In: Access the evaluation portal through your student login. Select Faculty: Choose the Faculty/Teacher on your class for which you wish to provide feedback. Rate and Comment: Evaluate various aspects, such as teaching effectiveness, course materials, and communication, through a user-friendly rating system and leave detailed comments. Submit Your Evaluation: Once you've completed the evaluation, submit your feedback securely.
